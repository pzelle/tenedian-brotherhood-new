Calendar of Events

Tenedian Brotherhood gatherings and Greek Orthodox feast days

About This Calendar

The original Brotherhood website featured an interactive Java applet calendar. Java applets are no longer supported by modern browsers. This page preserves the calendar's structure while noting the two traditional annual gatherings the Brotherhood has maintained since 1908.

For current event dates and announcements, please contact us.

Annual Brotherhood Traditions

Apokria

When: Last Sunday before Lent (February or March)

The Apokria celebration is held in the tradition of Mardi Gras — the last revelry before the Lenten fast. Members dress in costumes, play games, and enjoy Greek music and dancing together.

Saint Paraskevi Celebration

When: Sunday nearest July 26th

The summer gathering honors Saint Paraskevi, whose feast day falls on July 26th. Known for her charitable spirit, Saint Paraskevi is the ideal patroness for a Brotherhood founded on charitable giving to the homeland.
