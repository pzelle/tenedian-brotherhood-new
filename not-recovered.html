Help Restore Our History

If you have photos, recipes, program booklets, or other materials from Tenedian Brotherhood events — picnics, Apokria celebrations, family records, or anything else — we would love to preserve them here.

Please contact us and we can work together to restore this corner of our community's history.

What Was on the Original Site

The original Tenedian Brotherhood website (active approximately 2008–2015) contained dozens of photo albums, family records, recipe collections, event calendars, and commemorative pages. Among the content that was not archived:

  • Annual picnic photo albums
  • Apokria (pre-Lent) celebration photos
  • Marriage, baptism, and naturalization records
  • Cemetery and veterans' memorial pages
  • Pictures of Tenedos (Bozcaada)
  • Souvenir programs and Greek festival coverage
  • Food & wine recipe collections
  • Wedding and family photo albums
  • The St. Paraskevi celebration page
  • Most of the Just for Fun sub-pages

We have preserved everything that was captured, including the full text of the history, over 130 family trees, obituaries, and the Greek superstitions page.
